About

I have experience researching biomechanical case studies. I have previously used finite element analysis to simulate scoliosis bracing, and performed image analysis of myopic retinas to observe the effects of myopia on retinal shape. I am currently finishing my MPhil at QUT where I investigated whether Magnetic Resonance Elastography can measure intervertebral discs. I am looking to further my finite element knowledge and combine it with image analysis or mechanical analysis of biomechanical systems.

Research Projects

Validation of clinical MRE derived shear stiffness using mechanical testing of soft tissue phantoms and ovine intervertebral discs

Queensland University of Technology, Brisbane, Oct 2023-current

Undertaken as part of MPhil in Engineering, and comprised of:

  • Assessment of suitable analogues to use in magnetic resonance elastography
  • Development of protocol to validate MRE against shear mechanical testing
  • Validation by preparing and measuring silicone disks using MRE and mechanical testing
  • Verification by preparing and measuring ovine intervertebral discs using MRE and mechanical testing

Using Finite Element Analysis to analyse bracing in Adolescent Idiopathic Scoliosis

Queensland University of Technology, Brisbane, Oct 2023-Oct 2024

Undertaken as part of an attempt to articulate into PhD in Engineering, and comprised of:

  • Literature review on using finite element analysis to simulate the bracing in adolescent idiopathic scoliosis
  • Adapting an existing FEA model of the thoracolumbar region into one suitable for bracing
  • Assessment of suitable analogues to use in magnetic resonance elastography
  • Development of protocol to validate MRE against shear mechanical testing
  • Updated a MATLAB software to use for selecting landmarks on intervertebral discs

Detecting the True Ocular Shape of Myopic Eye and its association with Biomechanical Alterations

Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, Aug 2022-May 2023

Final Year Project as part of Bachelors in Bioengineering, comprising of:

  • Data processing of OCT scans to obtain the retinal shape of the eye.
  • Statistical analysis on retinal curvature to gain insight on the relationship between eye shape and myopia parameters.
  • Attempt of finite element analysis of the eye to derive a procedure to analyse the stresses on the retina.

Metallic and Magnetic Nanoparticles for the Inhibition of Amyloid Aggregation in Alzheimer’s disease

Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, Aug 2021-Jul 2022

URECA (Undergraduate Research Experience on Campus) project during Bachelors in Bioengineering, comprising of:

  • Synthesis of iron nanoparticles.
  • Assessment of nanoparticle's inhibition of amyloids using thioflavin T assays.
  • Gained insight into possible treatments for Alzheimer’s.

Computer-aided Design and 3D Printing of Adolescent Idiopathic Scoliosis Brace
